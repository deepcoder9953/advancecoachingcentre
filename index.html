FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the batch size at Advance Coaching Centre? +
We maintain a maximum of 35–50 students per batch to ensure individualised attention. Our student-to-teacher ratio is one of the best in Delhi NCR, allowing every student to get their doubts cleared effectively.
Do you offer scholarships or fee concessions? +
Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships of up to 100% fee waiver based on our admission test scores. We also have need-based financial assistance and sibling discounts. Call us at 8010211629 for details.
Is there an online mode of learning available? +
Yes! We offer a hybrid learning model where all live classes are recorded and available on our student portal. We also have dedicated online-only batches for students who cannot attend in person.
How often are tests and mock exams conducted? +
We conduct weekly chapter tests, bi-weekly unit tests, and monthly full mock exams. For JEE/NEET batches, we also conduct NTA-pattern simulated tests every Sunday. Detailed performance analysis is shared after each test.
Can I switch batches after enrollment? +
Yes, batch transfers are allowed within the first 30 days of joining, subject to seat availability in the desired batch. Please speak to our admin team or call 8010211629 to initiate a batch transfer request.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a full refund within 7 days of enrollment if you're not satisfied. After 7 days, a prorated refund policy applies based on classes attended. All refund requests must be submitted in writing to our admin office.
How can I speak to a counsellor? +
You can call or WhatsApp us directly at 8010211629 from Monday to Saturday between 7 AM and 9 PM. You can also fill the enquiry form on this page and our counsellors will call you back within 24 hours.
